About Villa Sunrise

Villa Sunrise is situated in a beautiful rural area of central Corfu, with a fantastic sea view overlooking the mountains of mainland Greece and Albania, perfect for an early morning sunrise.

Facilities
  • 3 bedrooms
  • 2 bathrooms
  • Open plan kitchen and dining and lounge area
  • Balconies all round, with great views and privacy
  • Gardens for relaxation and BBQ
  • Wi-Fi

Location

Located near Gouvia, and within easy reach of other popular destinations such as Dassia, Ipsos, Corfu Town and Aqualand.

Prices

Priced from 80 euros per night. Sleeps up to 6 persons.
